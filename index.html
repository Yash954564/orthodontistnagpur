Frequently Asked Questions

Got Questions?

Orthodontic correction can be performed at any age. The American Association of Orthodontists recommends a child's first screening by age 7, but adult corrections are highly successful using modern cosmetic solutions like clear aligners.

The cost of Invisalign clear aligners depends on the complexity of alignment needed and duration. We offer flexible zero-interest monthly installment plans to make clear aligner treatments affordable.

Clear aligners apply controlled gentle pressure to reposition teeth. Some initial tightness is expected on wearing a new tray set, but it is highly comfortable and does not irritate oral tissues compared to metal brackets.

Most standard orthodontic corrections take between 12 to 24 months. Mild spacing or crowding cases using clear aligners can often be successfully completed in 6 to 9 months.
